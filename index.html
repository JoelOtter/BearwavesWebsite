Team

The Bearwaves team may be small, but it has the talent of a large pack of bears. If you did not already know, bears are one of the most skilled creatures in the animal kingdom. Grizzly bears, one of the more active members of the bear family, have been known to create traps for their prey. They work in groups; the more bears in the group, the higher the yield of salmon.
